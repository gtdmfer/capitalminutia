Where am I going with all this?

Food Coach What you eat does create your baseline health. Crazy food prices and huge demands on your time make healthy eating impossible without an understanding of nutrition, modern agriculture practices - good and bad, and a set of skills in the kitchen and grocery store. Instill a routine for healthy eating now and continue to revive your health for your future years.

Regenerative Degrowth There is no doubt the fundamental philosophy of our economy has to change. For my part in forging a new way - I am collecting the excesses of Mother Culture and thrifting them at reasonable prices. Because used things should be cheap and useful things should not be trash.

Cohabitation as a New Social Contract If the exploitation of the modern economy were to be seen and eridicated, which way could we choose to organize society? Perhaps by agreeing to the fundamental principle of cohabitation: as beings that share this planet, we run the systems that make it easier for all beings to live safe, fed, and sheltered lives. Obviously this is in the beginning stages of taking shape, but this is what I hope to build up to, even though this is still in the thought experiment stage right now..
